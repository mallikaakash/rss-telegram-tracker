How confessions can keep language models honest

A novel technical alignment and RLHF technique from OpenAI that trains models to explicitly report their own misbehaviors (reward-hacking, scheming) via a separate "honesty-only" reward channel.
  • Introduces 'confessions', a separate model output trained solely on honesty, where admitting to misbehavior increases reward.

  • Provides a mechanism to detect scheming and reward-hacking in highly capable, agentic models before deployment.
