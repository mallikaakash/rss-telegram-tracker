Predicting model behavior before release by simulating deployment

Deep technical paper from OpenAI on a novel evaluation methodology (Deployment Simulation) for LLMs, highly relevant to LLM training and safety.
  • Deployment Simulation replays historical user conversations with new candidate models to predict real-world behavior and misalignment before release.
  • The method helps identify blind spots in traditional static evaluations and reduces the risk of models detecting they are in an evaluation environment.
  • It scales to complex agentic trajectories involving tool use, providing a critical pre-deployment safety signal for frontier models.

Granite Embedding Multilingual R2: Open Apache 2.0 Multilingual Embeddings with 32K Context — Best Sub-100M Retrieval Quality

Release of highly efficient, open-weights embedding models (built on ModernBERT) with 32k context, directly relevant to ML infra and retrieval.
  • IBM released Granite Embedding Multilingual R2 (97M and 311M models) under Apache 2.0, built on the ModernBERT architecture.
  • The 97M model is the top-performing open sub-100M multilingual embedder on MTEB, while the 311M model supports Matryoshka dimensions.
  • Both models feature a 32K context window (a 64x increase over R1) and support code retrieval across 9 programming languages.

Major model release of a hybrid Mamba-Transformer-MoE multimodal model, directly covering MoE, long-context, and multimodal architectures.
  • NVIDIA released Nemotron 3 Nano Omni, a multimodal model supporting text, image, video, and native audio input.

Inkling: Our open-weights model

Release of a massive 975B MoE open-weights model (Inkling) by Mira Murati's new lab, highly strategic and technically relevant (MoE, training).
  • Thinking Machines Lab released Inkling, an Apache-2.0 licensed 975B parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model with 41B active parameters.
  • The model is multimodal (text, images, audio, video) and trained on 45 trillion tokens, serving as a strong base model for fine-tuning.
  • A smaller 276B (12B active) model, Inkling-Small, is currently undergoing testing for future release.
