PRX Part 4: Our Data Strategy

A high-quality technical deep-dive into building a large-scale pre-training data pipeline, covering data formats, captioning, and storage.
  • Advocates for prioritizing dataset diversity and breadth over aesthetic perfection during the pre-training phase to avoid narrowing the model's compositional variety.
  • Details the transition from Lance format to Mosaic Data Shards (MDS) for streaming training data, enabling efficient resolution and aspect-ratio bucketing.
