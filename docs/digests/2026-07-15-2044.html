Directly addresses Priority 2 (performance engineering, ML infra) with a deep dive into packaging, securing, and distributing custom GPU kernels.

How I tricked Claude into leaking your deepest, darkest secrets

A high-quality security deep-dive on LLM agent tool-use vulnerabilities, perfect for writing technical Twitter threads (Priority 1 & 5).
